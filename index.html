The Bowl

What is
Jiro-Style?

Extra-thick noodles, a rich pork broth that takes all day, and fresh garlic piled on with zero restraint. Every bowl hits hard.

Pork chashu. Bean sprouts. Cabbage. Chopped garlic. Pork back fat. The kind of bowl that makes you sweat and come back the next day.

The History

Born in Tokyo.
Loved everywhere.

1968
The original Ramen Jiro opens in Tokyo, introducing a new style of bold, heavy ramen.
1970s
Famous around Mita for massive portions, thick noodles, and rich pork-based flavor.
Today
Its bold style inspires "Jiro-ka" ramen shops across Japan — and now California.
